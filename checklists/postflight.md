# Post-flight Checklist

- [ ] `PO-01` Final changed-file/artifact, requirement, decision, and finding inventories are complete.
- [ ] `PO-02` Affected modules and direct/transitive consumers are identified.
- [ ] `PO-03` Module registry, dependency graph, terminology, and decision index match final state.
- [ ] `PO-04` Active documentation, interfaces, examples, cross-references, and tests are synchronized.
- [ ] `PO-05` Superseded/historical material is labeled and no duplicate authority was created.
- [ ] `PO-06` Generated artifacts and lockfiles match their authoritative sources.
- [ ] `PO-07` No temporary bypass, debug artifact, placeholder, or unresolved blocker remains.
- [ ] `PO-08` Deferred non-blocking actions have owner, reason, due gate/date, and impact.
- [ ] `PO-09` Synchronization state and stale context indexes are updated/invalidated.
- [ ] `PO-10` G9 and terminal workflow state are recorded against the final revision.
