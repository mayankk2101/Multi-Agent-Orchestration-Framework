# Validation Checklist

- [ ] `VL-01` Candidate revision/version, frozen inputs, environment, and evidence manifest are exact.
- [ ] `VL-02` Every requirement and acceptance criterion maps to implementation and passing verification.
- [ ] `VL-03` Every business rule has authority and positive, negative, boundary, and precedence evidence as applicable.
- [ ] `VL-04` Ownership, state writers, dependencies, consumers, contracts, events, manifests, and graph agree.
- [ ] `VL-05` Implementation conforms to approved architecture and all architecture findings are resolved.
- [ ] `VL-06` Terminology and active documentation match implementation; no duplicate canonical rule exists.
- [ ] `VL-07` No normative behavior depends on an unsupported assumption or unresolved unknown.
- [ ] `VL-08` Applicable tests/static/build/migration/security/performance checks reproduce successfully.
- [ ] `VL-09` Constitution, workflow, review independence, and approval requirements are satisfied.
- [ ] `VL-10` Limitations, unrun checks, residual risks, and their gate effects are explicit.
